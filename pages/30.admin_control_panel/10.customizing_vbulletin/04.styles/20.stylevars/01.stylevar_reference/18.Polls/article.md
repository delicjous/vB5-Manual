## Poll Result Border

Controls the border width, style, and color for poll result bars.

- Variable Name: poll_result_border
- Data Type: border
- Default Value(s):
  - units: px
  - width: 1
  - style: solid
  - color: global_palette_fill_02 **(Inherited)**

- Template List: css_global.css
